GPX Viewer with Drive
The GPX viewer is made for the purpose of viewing the gpx file that you recorded.
You can load tracks from gpx file and show them on a Google Maps view. You can also see your GPS position on a map and use viewer as navigation.

This GPX Viewer does not save or share the file you select. It only works locally without going through the server. (HTML5 technology)
You can open a GPX file to view from your computer or your Google Drive.
With this viewer you can open a gpx file and view its contents very easily.
